Books

Our primary and most important book will always be the "Bible". The Holy Spirit should be the one who guides and reveals the true meaning of God's Word to us. While Christian books can be helpful, excessive dependence on them may take away valuable time that should be spent reading the Bible and receiving God's Word directly. Therefore, they should be used in moderation and never as a replacement for Scripture. Some books provide historical accounts of events that took place after the Bible was written, helping us understand how God's promises were fulfilled, particularly in Israel, including the rebirth of the modern State of Israel. The books listed here are intended to encourage you to read the Bible more deeply, strengthen your relationship with God, and grow in your faith. They are meant to be companions to your spiritual journey—not substitutes for God's Word.
